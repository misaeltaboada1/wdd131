Website Planning Document

FC Barcelona Fan Page

Description: A fan-based website dedicated to FC Barcelona, one of the most iconic football clubs in the world. The site highlights the club’s rich history, legendary players, current team lineup, stadium details, and latest achievements. It will also feature an image gallery, fan polls, and a contact form to interact with visitors.

Reason for Choice: As a passionate FC Barcelona fan, I wanted to design a space that celebrates the team’s history and culture while providing fans around the world with easy access to key information about the club.

Optional domain: fcbfanpage.com

Scenarios

  • A visitor wants to know who the current captain and key players of FC Barcelona are.
  • A fan is looking for images of Camp Nou and photos of recent victories.
  • Someone new to the club wants to learn about its history, founders, and greatest achievements.

Color Schema

The site uses a color scheme inspired by FC Barcelona’s traditional colors — deep blue and claret red — complemented by golden accents.

  • Primary Color (Barça Blue): – Used for the header, navigation bar, and key buttons.
  • Secondary Color (Barça Red): – Used for section titles, borders, and highlights.
  • Accent Color (Gold): – Used for small details, hover effects, and footer text.
  • Neutral Background: Light gray or white – Used to keep content areas clean and readable.

Typography

The website will use a clean and modern combination of fonts for readability and visual harmony.

  • Headings: Bebas Neue – for titles and navigation.
  • Body Text: Inter – elegant and easy to read for long paragraphs.
